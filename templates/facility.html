ADVANCED CENTRE

FOR

CRYO ELECTRON MICROSCOPY


Any academic or industrial researchers and scientists who want to use the ADVANCED CENTER FOR CRYO-ELECTRON MICROSCOPY FACILITY at IISc for high-resolution data collection should directly contact the EM manager (cryoem.iisc@gmail.com) or (somnath@iisc.ac.in). A nominal charge will be applicable for cryo-EM grid preparation & microscope usage. Users must provide sample details and preliminary purification or biochemical data to access the microscope. If not provided, no assurance will be given about grid/data quality.

Most cryo-EM apparatuses and accessories are expensive. Therefore, external/internal users cannot request cryo-freezing, cartridge preparation, or cassette transfer. Only expert users (mainly PIs) should prepare samples or hand over to the EM manager. Only trained users are allowed to operate the microscope (Latitude S Software). Data will be transferred to users' hard drives (not provided by the facility), and processing may take 2–3 days. Users should make their own arrangements for collecting/shipping the drives. While full data processing support is not available, assistance will be provided if necessary.

Users publishing work based on data collected at our facility should acknowledge the facility personnel and include the following funding acknowledgement:

“The data was collected at the ADVANCED CENTER FOR CRYO-ELECTRON MICROSCOPY FACILITY FOR BIOLOGICAL SCIENCES AT IISc, Bengaluru, India funded by Department of Biotechnology (DBT) Infrastructure Grant, Govt of India and DST-FIST.”

This applies even if the data collection is conducted elsewhere after screening at IISc. (somnath@iisc.ac.in)
